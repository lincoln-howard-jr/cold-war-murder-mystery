Character Brief: Agent Bartender
Name: Tom
Cover Name: Tom
Cover Career: Private-Craft Cocktail Bartender
Hometown: Fairfax, VA
Education: WT Woodson High School

Backstory
The truth is like alcohol. It's great in small doses.

People have always overlooked me, even if they can't look over me. Get it? It's funny because I'm tall. Yes, I did have to explain the joke because humor distracts you from my real identity. But seriously, people do overlook me.

That has always worked in my favor. At every embassy function, diplomatic dinner, and private club event where I've stood behind a bar, secrets served within arm's reach while nobody noticed the person carrying them. I was recruited by the FBI precisely because people underestimate someone with a tray.

Tonight I am one man in a black vest with a tray and no invitation card printed in ink. I am monitoring the room, gathering signals, and confirming whether two suspected KGB operatives are present. If they are, they will not get a clean exit.

The truth is best saved for the last pour.

Objective
Remain hidden as federal law enforcement until the last possible moment. When the time comes, disclose you are an FBI agent and arrest both KGB agents.
