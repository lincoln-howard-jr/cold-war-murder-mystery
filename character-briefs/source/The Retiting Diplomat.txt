Character Brief: The Retiring Diplomat
Name: Charles Whitmore
Age: 67
Career: Ambassador
Hometown: Greenwich, Connecticut
Education: Graduated Summa Cum Laude with a BA in History with a concentration on diplomatic history.
Backstory
I am a polarizing figure in modern politics — a powerful man who protected the interests of the United States, yet, to some, a selfish political pawn with deep ties to defense contractors.

My crowning achievement was the Strategic Stability Accord. The Accord, among other critical provisions, limited the development of certain classes of weapons — nuclear, biological, chemical, and deep-space systems — while permitting the modernization of others. It was not a perfect agreement, but one that allows us to survive.

My critics will say that Aegis Dynamics Group invested millions in lobbying efforts surrounding the deal. Every dollar was legal. Every meeting was disclosed. Every channel was proper.

My forty years in the geopolitical arena paid dividends for the world as much as they did for me. The Strategic Stability Accord achieved a long-term peace that many believed was impossible until I brought the right people to the right table at the right moment.

There are those who would like to stain my legacy. There always are. Peace is profitable for some and threatening to others. My wife and I planned this evening carefully. This celebration is not merely a retirement party — it is a reminder. I am the diplomat who brought peace to the world. And history will remember me as such.
Objective
It's your night, live it up... or die trying!
