Character Brief: The Successor
Name: Talia Jane
Age: 39
Career: Ambassador-Designate, U.S. State Department
Hometown: Arlington, Virginia
Education: Georgetown University School of Foreign Service
Backstory
I have always had a way with words. But for every eloquently worded statement is an equal disdain for the system I've been so fortunate to benefit from.
Charles Whitmore selected me to inherit his diplomatic connections. Publicly, I am his clean continuation. Privately, I am something else entirely.
Years ago, while posted abroad, I was approached by Soviet handlers who understood my ambition better than my own colleagues did. They offered an ideological home, people that care about the equitability of society more than the lining of their own pockets.

When I returned to the States, I was greeted with the Strategic Stability Accord, a corrupt deal with Aegis Dynamics being the primary benefactor. How could Charles have been so instrumental in something so tragic? I will be a better negotiator than he ever was, more principled and more just.

Tonight is a transfer of power disguised as a celebration. If I perform well, I walk out as the trusted moderate successor.

My handlers have arranged a split drop to protect the network. One agent carries the intelligence itself, encrypted with a basic cipher. A second agent carries only the cipher key. Individually, each piece is meaningless. Together, they could decide who rises, who falls, and what story survives the night.

Objective
Before the midnight toast, obtain both halves of your KGB drop from two unknown contacts: the encrypted intelligence note from one and the cipher key from the other. Decode the message and use what you learn to strengthen your path to succession, while preserving your public image as a calm moderate.
Connections

Anna Whitmore (Jana) — She sees you as polished and useful, but she is perceptive enough to notice cracks in your facade.

Sam Jackson (Elliot) — A respected columnist whose opinion can legitimize your rise if he trusts your character.
